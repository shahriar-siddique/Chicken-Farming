Acivet Cipro® 20% (Vet)

        Composition
        Each 100 g powder contains:
        Ciprofloxacin Hydrochloride USP 22.20 g equivalent to 20 g Ciprofloxacin
        
        Indications
        Acivet Cipro® 20% Oral Powder effective against most of the Bacterial & Mycoplasmal diseases like C.R.D., Mycoplasmosis, Salmonellosis, Colibacillosis, Pasteurellosis, Infectious Coryza, Necrotic Enteritis, Staphylococcosis and secondary bacterial Infections in case of viral diseases (like Gumboro, Ranikhet etc.)
        
        Dosage & Administration
        Ciprofloxacin is normally used at the rate of 5-15 mg/kg body weight.
        
        Prevention: 1 g per 8 liters of drinking water for 3-5 consecutive days.
        Treatment: 1 g per 2-4 liters of drinking water for 3-5 consecutive days.
        
        “Use only advised by the registered veterinarian” and “should not be used above the recommended dosage”.
        
        Withdrawal Period
        7 days
        
        Storage
        Store in a cool and dry place protected from light.
        Keep out of the reach of children.
        
        Pack Size
        500 gm
