DLC-Vet Powder

        Composition
        Each 100 gm contains:
        
        Methionine               40 mg
        Lysine                      25 mg
        Choline Chloride      10 mg
        Biotin                      25 mcg
        Selenium               1000 mcg
        Vitamin                  A 250000 IU
        Vitamin                 D3 30000 IU
        Vitamin                 E 150000 IU
        Vitamin                 B12 1000 mcg
        Calcium Acetate        10 mg
        Lactose q.s to           100 gm
        
        Indication
        ♣ Stimulation of natural immunity, prevent Liver & kidney damage
        ♣ Prevent leg weakness, fatty liver syndrome & emaciation (loss of fatty tissue)
        ♣ For correction of nutritional diseases such as vitamin, protein & mineral disorder in cattle & poultry
        ♣ Maintain overall health & reproductive health
        ♣ Improve fertility & milk production of cattle
        ♣ Prevents early chicks infections & mortality
        ♣ Improves egg production and overcome stress & mineral deficiency
        ♣ Boost up instant energy after stress or disease
        ♣ Improve FCR, maintain metabolism & healthy body tissue
        
        Dosage & Administration
        Dosages should be administered for 7 to 10 days per month or recommended by veterinary physician
        
        Chick: 50 gm/100 kg feed
        Broiler & grower: 75 gm/100 kg feed
        Layer: 100 gm/100 kg feed
        Cow & Bualo: 50 gm daily
        Small animal: 5 gm daily
        Dog & Cat: 1 gm daily
        
        Pack Size
        500 g
        
        Storage
        Store in a cool & dry place protected from light. Keep out of reach of children.
